Activities

We offer a wide variety of activities. At Greywood Trails Outdoor Adventures we have a great river for fishing and kayaking. There are many beautiful hiking trails. We offer horseback riding as well as rock climbing. A new wilderness survival class is now offered.

Activity Description Cost
Fishing Fishing is available at our beautiful river. Fishing supplies are available for rent. Fishing license required. $15/day
Hiking Go hiking on one of our many, scenic trails. A variety of skill level trails are available. Free
Horseback Riding Horseback riding is available with one of our trained staff members. Enjoy a relaxing ride in nature. $30/hour
*$15/additional hour
Kayaking Go kayaking with friends and family down a 3-mile route. Kayaking supplies are available for rent. Buses are available for your return trip. $25/hour
*$12/additional hour
Rock Climbing Enjoy our outdoor rock climbing course. This is great for those who love a challenge. Gear is available for rent. $65/day
**New**
Wilderness Survival Class		 Our newly added wilderness survival classes will prepare you for skills necessary for the great outdoors. See front desk for sign up. $75/class
